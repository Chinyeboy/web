About Kachenda Daycare

Madam Rhoda

Founder & Director

Our Story

I am a very polite and charming woman who really loves to live in peace and happiness with children and all the workers.

I always love playing and teaching in my daycare, but also drawing and painting is part of our activities with the children which is quite fun and enjoyable.

Apart from being a teacher in my daycare, I am also a social worker and mother. Many people thank me because I'm a woman who really takes care of their children like how I take care of my own.

Our History

Kachenda Daycare was founded on June 29, 2020. It began with only five children and three teachers including me. We didn't have much equipment to practice teaching, but we got some help from our friends outside the country.

The support didn't stop there - we received sponsorship from an organization called VIVA TANZANIA. This organization supported us by inviting visitors from outside the country to come and support us. Through that, we increased from five children to fifteen children.

The purpose of this daycare is to help parents and all people to have more time to do their jobs and business in a very peaceful way without any worries. This has been my dream since I was a little girl, and I thank God for it becoming a successful reality.

My first job was at a place called ST. STEPHEN CHILDREN CENTER (COMPERSSION). I started working there on September 7, 1999.
